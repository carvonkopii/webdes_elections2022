2022 National Elections

Registered voters will cast ballots on May 9, 2022, to elect officials to executive and legislative positions at the national, provincial, and local levels.

In total, the Filipinos will elect a candidate for each:
  • President
  • Vice President
  • Senators
  • Mayors and Vice-Mayors of Cities and Municipalities
  • Councilors
